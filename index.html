CANTO I

Silent Tears

CANTO VI

The City of Strangers

CANTO VII

The City of Strangers

In this city of strangers… every face is a story untold, every glance a whisper of loneliness. The rain doesn’t merely fall here— it mourns, it remembers the dreams we once carried, the hearts we once lost. People pass like shadows, hands almost touching, eyes almost meeting, souls almost speaking… Hope is fragile here, a glass balanced on the edge of despair, yet even in emptiness, hearts search— for a hand that will hold, for a voice that will stay, for a stranger who feels like home. The city teaches you quietly: loneliness is not the absence of people… but the presence of unspoken truths. And sometimes, in the silence of streets, even strangers become mirrors of our own longing.

The Mirror of Souls

And perhaps… just perhaps… the city of strangers is a mirror of our own souls, reflecting the longing, the grief, and the beauty we carry within.
